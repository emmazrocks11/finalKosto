Biography

She is the gold medalist of the 2022 Winter Olympic champion in women's singles, the 2021 World champion, the 2022 European champion, and a three-time Russian national champion (2019–2021). Along with the rest of TripleA, Shcherbakova set world records in women's figure skating with quadruple jumps. She is the only skater out of TripleA that has never left their original coach—Eteri Tutberidze.
